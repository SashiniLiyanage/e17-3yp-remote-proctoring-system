PHASE 4 : Hardware implementation

HARDWARE OVERVIEW


The proctoring device in the student's side has 2 main components.

  • The raspberry pi 3B+ module with a touch screen,camera,microphone and speaker connected.
  • Power Supply unit

Main circuit of the device

Circuit diagram of the proctoring device on the student's side

Components

  • Raspberry Pi 3B+
  • LCD Touch Screen
  • Raspberry Pi Camera
  • USB Microphone
  • Speaker
  • Micro SD card
  • Cooling fan

Raspberry Pi 3B+

  • 5V/2.5A DC power input
  • Built-in wifi module
  • Micro SD port
  • CSI camera port
  • Full-size HDMI

LCD Touch Screen

  • 800 x 480 HD resolution
  • HDMI interface for displaying
  • USB interface for touch control
  • Supports the backlight control to save the electricity

Raspberry Pi Camera

  • 5 megapixel camera
  • Pixel Count 2592 x 1944
  • Capture video at 1080p30 with H.264 encoding
  • Angle of View 54 x 41 degrees
  • Field of View 2.0 x 1.33 m at 2 m

USB Microphone

  • Driver-free
  • Plug and play
  • Frequency response:100-16kHz

Speaker

  • Diameter 2.9cm Loud Speaker
  • Amplifier board 5W x2 Stereo

Micro SD card

  • 32 GB

Cooling fan

Power Supply unit

Circuit diagram of the power supply unit

  • The power supply unit has three Li-ion batteries to supply power to the main unit incase of a power failure.
  • The 3S charging board regulates the voltage supplied to the raspberry pi unit and cuts off supply when the batteries are discharging below the average value.
  • The adjustable step-down power supply module supplies a constant 5V power supply to the raspberry pi board

Components

  • Lithium-ion 18650 batteries
  • 3S charging board
  • Adjustable step-down power Supply module
  • 12V DC power jack
  • Micro USB jack

Lithium-ion 18650 batteries

  • Capacity 1200mAh
  • Average voltage rating - 3.7V

3S charging board

  • Charging and discharging protection

Adjustable step-down power Supply module
