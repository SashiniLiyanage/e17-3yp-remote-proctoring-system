How to join to an examination room?

  1. Go to 'Exam Schedule' tab on the menu
  2. Find the exam on the calendar you want to join
  3. Change your name to join the exam (optional)
  4. Click on the JOIN button (button is only displayed one hour before the exam

Exam room configurations

  1. Mute everyone except your invigilator

    Click on the menu icon on the Invigilators video and click "Mute everyone else"



    Click on "Mute" to mute everyone except the invigilator




  2. Adjust volume of an individual

    Click on the menu icon of an examinee and adujust volume

    Warning: do not change your invigilators' volume to zero




  3. Send Private messages

    Click on the menu icon and click on the "Send Private message"

    Type the message on the chat and click on send button




How to upload saved videos?
  1. Go to 'Upload videos' tab on the menu
  2. Click on the video you want to upload
  3. Video will be uploaded automatically. This may take some time.
    Video upload path is specified by the administrator of the server and may change from one examination to another. If you have any doubts or issues, please contact the administrator of your connected server.
  4. Video will be deleted from your device after uploading is done.
How to test audio and video?
  1. Go to 'Settings' tab on the menu
  2. Click on the 'Test your camera' tab
  3. Click on the RECORD button and check your video quality
  4. Click on the PLAY button and check your audio level
